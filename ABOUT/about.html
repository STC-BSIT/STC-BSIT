COLLEGE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Vision

The Information Technology Department is envisioned as the College's instrument in bridging the digital division between the College's industry and community, offering computing sciences programs committed to produce competent and values oriented graduates as agents of progress in leveraging information technology.

Mission

The College of Information Technology department is committed to provide quality technology services and support, thereby enhancing the operation, management, research, and educational capabilities of the institution in order to equip its graduates with globally-relevant knowledge and skills through excellent training and innovative people management.

Goals

  • Provide the Students which a solid foundation of knowledge and understanding of the computing discipline as embodied in the course program.

  • Produce qualified and well-rounded computer professionals, academics and entrepreneurs who can develop systems supportive of the information needs of industry and government using computer based data processing system.

  • Provide sufficient exposure to relate academic instruction to actual practice and researches in the computing profession.

  • Produce student who embody the ideals of competence and quality consciousness; and

  • Continuously upgrade the skills and competence of students and faculty members to keep them abreast of the current development in the world of computers.
